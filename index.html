About Me

I develop solutions for problems with high technological and mathematical complexity, focusing on Artificial Intelligence (especially Machine Learning), edge computing, and cloud computing (with IoT-like architectures). My process consists of researching, testing, and integrating the best existing technologies to efficiently build new solutions, with the ability to develop proprietary algorithms and technologies when necessary.

With a solid background in engineering and computing, complemented by teaching experience, I developed strong skills across three pillars: cutting-edge technology development, knowledge sharing, and team leadership. This hybrid approach has shaped my academic career, where I've always worked simultaneously with systems development and research.Currently, I focus on developing applications and MVPs to demonstrate my implementation capabilities. My main stack includes Python, Flask, Django, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and LangChain. However, I emphasize that I'm technology agnostic, always selecting the most appropriate tool to solve the problem at hand.
