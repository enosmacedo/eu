About Me

I am a researcher and developer who thrives on technological challenges with high mathematical complexity. My specialty is creating algorithms and solutions in the Artificial Intelligence spectrum, with an emphasis on Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I have strong experience in integrating embedded systems with the cloud and the edge, developing robust IoT-like architectures. My work method is pragmatic: instead of reinventing the wheel, I search for, test, and integrate the best existing solutions to build new technologies efficiently.

My solid background in engineering and computer science, combined with my teaching experience, reflects a dual passion: developing cutting-edge technologies and sharing knowledge. Currently, I am dedicated to researching new AI algorithms and developing applications (MVPs) that not only solve problems but also strengthen my portfolio. This balance between theory and practice allows me to innovate consistently and with a strong foundation.
